Tallarines verde and chicken

Tallarines Verdes, or green noodles, is a delicious Peruvian dish that features a green sauce made with basil, spinach, and other herbs. Paired with chicken, it creates a flavorful and satisfying meal. Here's a simple recipe for Tallarines Verdes with Chicken:

Ingredients:

For the Tallarines Verdes Sauce:

2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed

1 cup fresh spinach leaves, packed

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1/2 cup walnuts or pine nuts

2 cloves garlic, peeled

1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

For the Chicken:

4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Tallarines (Noodles):

1 pound fettuccine or your favorite pasta

Instructions:

1.Prepare the Tallarines Verdes Sauce:

In a food processor, combine basil, spinach, Parmesan cheese, nuts, and garlic.

Pulse until ingredients are finely chopped.

With the processor running, gradually add the olive oil until the mixture becomes a smooth sauce.

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.

2.Cook the Chicken:

Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.

Cook chicken breasts for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked and no longer pink in the center.

Remove from heat and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.

3.Cook the Tallarines:

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.

Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.

4.Combine and Serve:

Pour the Tallarines Verdes sauce over the cooked pasta and toss until well coated.

Slice the cooked chicken breasts and arrange them on top of the pasta.

Serve immediately, garnishing with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Note:

This Tallarines Verdes and Chicken recipe offers a delightful blend of flavors from the herbaceous green sauce and the savory chicken. Feel free to adjust the ingredients and quantities based on your preferences. Enjoy your delicious Peruvian-inspired meal!

Cheesy Vegetarian One-Pot Pasta Recipe

This cheesy vegetarian one-pot pasta recipe is a perfect 30-minute weeknight meal! Loaded with vegetables and budget-friendly!

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 onion, finely chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 bell pepper, sliced

1 zucchini, sliced

8 oz (about 225g) pasta (penne, fusilli, or your choice)

2 1/2 cups vegetable broth

1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes (with juices)

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoon dried basil

1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for some heat)

1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1.Saute Vegetables:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, sauté until softened.

2.Add Vegetables and Pasta:

Add bell pepper and zucchini to the pot. Stir in the pasta, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes (with juices), oregano, basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper.

3.Simmer:

Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to simmer. Cover and cook for about 12-15 minutes or until the pasta is cooked and most of the liquid is absorbed, stirring occasionally.

4.Cheese Time:

Once the pasta is cooked, stir in the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese until melted and well combined.

5.Garnish and Serve:

Remove from heat. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley.

6.Serve:

Serve the cheesy vegetarian one-pot pasta immediately, and enjoy your comforting and flavorful meal!

Note:

Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the level of spiciness. This recipe is not only tasty but also convenient for a quick and easy weeknight meal.

Bolognese Pasta

Certainly! Here's a classic Bolognese Pasta recipe that yields a rich and flavorful meat sauce. This recipe is typically served with tagliatelle pasta, but you can use your favorite pasta variety.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 onion, finely chopped

2 carrots, peeled and finely chopped

2 celery stalks, finely chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 pound (450g) ground beef (or a mixture of pork and beef)

1/2 cup red wine (optional)

1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes

2 tablespoons tomato paste

1 bay leaf

1 teaspoon dried oregano

Salt and pepper to taste

Pinch of nutmeg (optional)

Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

1.Saute Vegetables:

In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, carrots, and celery. Saute until the vegetables are softened.

2.Add Garlic and Meat:

Add minced garlic and continue to sauté for another minute until fragrant. Add the ground beef, breaking it apart with a spoon, and brown it until cooked through.

3.Deglaze with Wine (optional):

Pour in the red wine, if using, and allow it to simmer until the liquid is reduced by half.

4.Add Tomatoes and Tomato Paste:

Stir in the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Mix well to combine.

5.Simmer with Milk and Seasonings:

Pour in the milk, add the bay leaf, oregano, salt, pepper, and nutmeg (if using). Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

6.Simmer and Cook:

Reduce the heat to low and let the Bolognese sauce simmer for at least 1.5 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally. The longer it cooks, the richer the flavors will be.

7.Adjust Seasoning:

Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Remove the bay leaf.

8.Cook Pasta:

While the sauce is simmering, cook your pasta according to the package instructions.

9.Serve:

Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked pasta. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley and grated Parmesan cheese.

Note:

This Bolognese Pasta recipe is a hearty and satisfying dish. Enjoy the depth of flavors that develop as the sauce simmers.

Fettuccine Alfredo With Shrimp Recipe

Whisk the Parmigiano-Reggiano into the sauce. Add the shrimp and cooked pasta, and toss well. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot in heated bowls.

Ingredients:

8 oz (about 225g) fettuccine pasta

1 pound (about 450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined

2 tablespoons olive oil

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup heavy cream

1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1/2 cup unsalted butter

Salt and black pepper to taste

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

1.Cook the Fettuccine:

Cook the fettuccine pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

2.Prepare the Shrimp:

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side or until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

3.Make the Alfredo Sauce:

In the same skillet, add the butter and allow it to melt.

Pour in the heavy cream, stirring continuously to combine with the melted butter.

Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.

4.Combine Shrimp and Alfredo Sauce:

Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce. Stir to coat the shrimp in the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the shrimp are heated through.

5.Combine with Fettuccine:

Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet, tossing it with the Alfredo sauce and shrimp until the pasta is well coated.

6.Season and Garnish:

Season the dish with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir in fresh chopped parsley for a burst of color and flavor.

7.Serve:

Serve the Fettuccine Alfredo with Shrimp immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and parsley if desired.

Note:

This creamy Fettuccine Alfredo with Shrimp is a decadent and satisfying dish that's perfect for special occasions or when you're craving a comforting pasta meal. Enjoy!

Mushroom Stroganoff with fluffy mashed potato

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients:

1 pound (about 450g) mushrooms, sliced (button or cremini mushrooms work well)

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion, finely chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 cup vegetable broth

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

1 cup sour cream

Salt and black pepper to taste

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

1.Sauté Mushrooms:

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and become golden brown. Remove half of the mushrooms and set them aside for later.

2.Cook Onions and Garlic:

In the same skillet, add chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions are translucent

3.Make the Sauce:

Sprinkle flour over the onion and garlic mixture and stir to combine. Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.

Gradually whisk in the vegetable broth, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard. Bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring continuously until it thickens.

Combine with Mushrooms:

Add back the reserved sautéed mushrooms to the skillet. Stir in sour cream and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Fluffy Mashed Potatoes:

Ingredients:

4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks

4 tablespoons unsalted butter

1/2 cup milk (or more for desired consistency)

Salt and white pepper to taste

Chopped chives or green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1.Boil Potatoes:

Place the potato chunks in a pot and cover with cold, salted water. Bring to a boil and simmer until the potatoes are fork-tender.

2.Mash Potatoes:

Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Mash with a potato masher or fork.

Add butter and milk gradually while mashing until you achieve the desired creamy consistency.

Season and Garnish:

Season the mashed potatoes with salt and white pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped chives or green onions if desired

Serve:

Serve the Mushroom Stroganoff over a bed of fluffy mashed potatoes. Garnish with fresh parsley on the stroganoff and enjoy your comforting and flavorful meal!

French Style Braised Short Ribs

Braised short ribs are a classic and comforting dish. Here's a French-style braised short ribs recipe that's rich and flavorful:

Ingredients:

4 pounds (about 1.8 kg) beef short ribs, bone-in

Salt and black pepper to taste

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 large onion, chopped

2 carrots, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 bottle (750 ml) red wine (such as Cabernet Sauvignon)

2 cups beef broth

2 tablespoons tomato paste

2 sprigs fresh thyme

2 bay leaves

1 tablespoon butter (optional, for finishing)

Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1.Preheat Oven:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

2.Season and Sear Short Ribs:

Season the short ribs generously with salt and black pepper. In a large oven-safe Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the short ribs on all sides until browned. Work in batches if necessary. Remove the ribs and set them aside.

3.Sauté Vegetables:

In the same pot, add chopped onions and carrots. Sauté until the vegetables are softened. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute

4.Deglaze the Pot:

Sprinkle flour over the vegetables and stir to combine. Cook for a minute to remove the raw flour taste. Pour in the red wine, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits.

5.Add Remaining Ingredients:

Return the seared short ribs to the pot. Add beef broth, tomato paste, fresh thyme, and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

6.Braise in the Oven:

Cover the Dutch oven with a lid and transfer it to the preheated oven. Braise for 2.5 to 3 hours or until the meat is fork-tender.

7.Finish and Serve:

Once done, remove the pot from the oven. Optionally, stir in a tablespoon of butter to add richness to the sauce. Discard the thyme sprigs and bay leaves.

Garnish and Serve:

Serve the braised short ribs over mashed potatoes, polenta, or a bed of egg noodles. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

note:

This French-style braised short ribs recipe results in tender, flavorful meat with a rich sauce. It's a perfect dish for a special occasion or when you want a hearty and comforting meal.

Beef Burgundy Recipe

Beef Burgundy, also known as Boeuf Bourguignon, is a classic French dish known for its rich and savory flavors. Here's a delicious Beef Burgundy recipe for you to try:

Ingredients:

2.5 pounds (about 1.1 kg) beef stew meat, cut into chunks

Salt and black pepper to taste

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 large onion, finely chopped

2 carrots, sliced

3 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 bottle (750 ml) red wine (such as Burgundy or Pinot Noir)

2 cups beef broth

2 tablespoons tomato paste

1 bouquet garni (a bundle of fresh thyme, parsley, and a bay leaf, tied together with kitchen twine)

8 ounces (about 225g) mushrooms, quartered

1 cup pearl onions, peeled

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

1.Preheat Oven:

Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).

2.Season and Brown the Beef:

Season the beef chunks with salt and black pepper. In a large oven-safe pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Brown the beef in batches to avoid overcrowding the pot. Once browned, set the beef aside.

3.Sauté Vegetables:

In the same pot, add chopped onions and sliced carrots. Sauté until the vegetables are softened. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

4.Add Flour:

Sprinkle flour over the vegetables and stir to combine. Cook for a minute to remove the raw flour taste.

5.Deglaze the Pot:

Pour in the red wine, scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits. This step adds depth of flavor to the dish.

6.Return the browned beef to the pot. Add beef broth, tomato paste, and the bouquet garni. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

Return the browned beef to the pot. Add beef broth, tomato paste, and the bouquet garni. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

7.Braise in the Oven:

Cover the pot with a lid and transfer it to the preheated oven. Braise for 2.5 to 3 hours or until the beef is fork-tender.

8.Sauté Mushrooms and Pearl Onions:

In a separate skillet, sauté quartered mushrooms and peeled pearl onions in a bit of olive oil until they are browned and cooked through.

9.Finish and Serve:

Once done, remove the pot from the oven. Discard the bouquet garni. Stir in the sautéed mushrooms and pearl onions.

10.Garnish and Serve:

Garnish the Beef Burgundy with chopped fresh parsley and serve over mashed potatoes, rice, or crusty bread.

note:

This Beef Burgundy recipe is a classic and comforting dish that's perfect for a special occasion or a cozy dinner. Enjoy the rich flavors and tender beef!

Easy Homemade Beef Stew

This Classic Homemade Beef Stew recipe is so easy to make and so heavenly. A one-pot meal that’s hearty, flavorful and loaded with tender beef morsels, potatoes, and carrots. Cooked in a rich and savory sauce, this beef stew recipe is comfort food at its best.

Ingredients:

2 pounds (about 900g) stew beef, cut into bite-sized pieces

Salt and black pepper to taste

1/3 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 large onion, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

4 cups beef broth

1 cup red wine (optional, can substitute with additional beef broth)

2 bay leaves

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried rosemary

4 large carrots, peeled and cut into chunks

3 potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks

1 cup frozen peas (optional)

Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1.Season and Coat Beef:

Season the stew beef with salt and black pepper. Dredge the beef in flour, shaking off any excess.

2.Brown the Beef:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Brown the beef in batches to ensure even browning. Set aside the browned beef.

3.Sauté Onions and Garlic:

In the same pot, add chopped onions and sauté until softened. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

4.Deglaze the Pot:

Pour in the beef broth and red wine (if using), scraping the bottom of the pot to release any browned bits.

5.Combine Ingredients:

Return the browned beef to the pot. Add bay leaves, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

6.Simmer the Stew:

Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let the stew simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is tender.

7.Add Vegetables:

Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot. Simmer for an additional 30-45 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked through and the flavors meld.

8.Optional: Add Peas:

If using frozen peas, add them to the stew in the last 5-10 minutes of cooking.

9.Adjust Seasoning:

Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and black pepper as needed.

Garnish and Serve:

Garnish and Serve:

Note:

This easy homemade beef stew is hearty, comforting, and perfect for colder days. Feel free to customize it by adding your favorite vegetables or herbs. Enjoy!

Creamy Garlic Chicken Gnocchi Soup

This Creamy Garlic Chicken Gnocchi Soup is better than Olive Garden and is sure to keep you warm and comforted.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 pound (about 450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced

Salt and black pepper to taste

4 tablespoons unsalted butter

1 onion, finely chopped

4 cloves garlic, minced

1/4 cup all-purpose flour

4 cups chicken broth

1 cup milk

1 cup half-and-half

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 pound (about 450g) potato gnocchi

2 cups fresh spinach leaves, chopped

1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Salt and black pepper to taste

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

1.Cook Chicken:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Season diced chicken with salt and black pepper. Cook the chicken until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set it aside.

2.Sauté Onions and Garlic:

In the same pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until softened. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

3.Make Roux:

Sprinkle flour over the onion and garlic mixture, stirring constantly to form a roux. Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.

4.Add Broth and Milk:

Gradually whisk in chicken broth, milk, and half-and-half. Bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring continuously.

5.Add Seasonings and Gnocchi:

Stir in dried thyme, dried oregano, and the cooked chicken. Add the potato gnocchi to the pot and cook according to the package instructions.

6.Add Spinach and Parmesan:

Once the gnocchi is cooked, add the chopped spinach and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir until the spinach wilts and the cheese is melted.

7.Season to Taste:

Season the soup with salt and black pepper to taste.

8.Garnish and Serve:

Garnish the creamy garlic chicken gnocchi soup with chopped fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy!

Note:

This creamy soup is a comforting and satisfying meal, perfect for chilly days. Feel free to customize it by adding other vegetables or herbs according to your preference.

One-Pot Creamy Vegetable Soup

This creamy vegetable soup can be made in just over 30 minutes and everything cooks together all in one pot for a quick and easy weeknight dinner that your family will love! Switch up the veggies to suit your taste or use it as a "clean out the fridge" meal to get rid of leftovers and odds and ends!

Ingredients:

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion, chopped

2 carrots, peeled and sliced

2 celery stalks, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoon dried rosemary

4 cups vegetable broth

2 cups water

2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced

1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces

1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)

1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)

Salt and black pepper to taste

1 cup heavy cream or half-and-half

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions:

1.Sauté Vegetables:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, carrots, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.

2.Add Aromatics:

Add minced garlic, dried thyme, dried oregano, and dried rosemary. Stir and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

3.Add Broth and Water:

Pour in the vegetable broth and water. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

4.Add Potatoes and Vegetables:

Add diced potatoes, green beans, corn, and peas to the pot. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.

5.Season:

Season the soup with salt and black pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.

6.Add Cream:

Stir in the heavy cream or half-and-half. Allow the soup to heat through, but do not boil once the cream is added.

7.Garnish and Serve:

Garnish the creamy vegetable soup with chopped fresh parsley, if desired. Serve hot, and optionally, top with grated Parmesan cheese.

Note:

This one-pot creamy vegetable soup is versatile, and you can customize it by adding your favorite vegetables or herbs. It's a comforting and satisfying soup that's perfect for a cozy meal. Enjoy!

The Coziest Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe for Winter

This homemade chicken noodle soup recipe is made with homestyle egg noodles, thick-cut veggies and the coziest garlic-herb broth. Delicious!

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 onion, finely chopped

3 carrots, sliced

3 celery stalks, sliced

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 bay leaf

8 cups chicken broth (homemade or store-bought)

2 cups shredded cooked chicken (rotisserie chicken works well)

2 cups egg noodles

Salt and black pepper to taste

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Lemon wedges for serving (optional)

Instructions:

1.Sauté Aromatics:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, sliced carrots, and sliced celery. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.

2.Add Garlic and Herbs:

Add minced garlic, dried thyme, and the bay leaf. Stir and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

3.Pour in Chicken Broth:

Pour in the chicken broth, bringing the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to simmer for about 10-15 minutes to let the flavors meld.

4.Add Shredded Chicken:

Stir in the shredded cooked chicken, allowing it to heat through in the broth.

5.Cook Egg Noodles:

Add the egg noodles to the pot and cook according to the package instructions, usually for about 6-8 minutes.

6.Season to Taste:

Season the soup with salt and black pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.

7.Serve:

Remove the bay leaf. Ladle the cozy chicken noodle soup into bowls. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

8.Optional: Lemon Wedges:

Serve with lemon wedges on the side for a burst of freshness (optional).

Note:

This Chicken Noodle Soup is the epitome of comfort and warmth, making it perfect for winter days or whenever you need a soothing meal. Feel free to customize the recipe by adding additional vegetables or herbs based on your preferences. Enjoy!

Vegetable Soup

This homemade vegetable soup recipe is hearty, healthy, easy to make, and tastes fantastic. It's also vegan when you use vegetable broth!

Ingredients:

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion, diced

3 carrots, peeled and sliced

3 celery stalks, sliced

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 zucchini, diced

1 yellow squash, diced

1 bell pepper, diced (any color)

1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained

6 cups vegetable broth

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 bay leaf

Salt and black pepper to taste

1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces

1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)

1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)

1 cup small pasta (such as macaroni or small shells)

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

1.Sauté Vegetables:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onions, sliced carrots, sliced celery, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.

2.Add Zucchini, Yellow Squash, and Bell Pepper:

Add diced zucchini, diced yellow squash, and diced bell pepper to the pot. Sauté for an additional 3-5 minutes.

3.Add Tomatoes and Broth:

Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juices and vegetable broth. Stir to combine.

4.Season:

Add dried thyme, dried oregano, bay leaf, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir well.

5.Simmer:

Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

6.Add Green Beans, Corn, Peas, and Pasta:

Add green beans, corn, peas, and small pasta to the pot. Continue to simmer until the pasta is cooked and the vegetables are tender.

7.Adjust Seasoning:

Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Remove the bay leaf.

8.Serve:

Ladle the vegetable soup into bowls. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

Note:

This vegetable soup is a versatile recipe, and you can customize it by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the seasoning. It's a nutritious and comforting dish, perfect for a wholesome meal. Enjoy!

Garlic Herb Roasted Potatoes Carrots and Zucchini

This simple veggie blend of potatoes, carrots, and zucchini is seasoned with delicious garlic and fresh herb mix then roasted to maximum deliciousness

Ingredients:

1.5 pounds (about 680g) baby potatoes, halved or quartered

1 pound (about 450g) baby carrots

2 medium zucchini, sliced into rounds or half-moons

3 tablespoons olive oil

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried rosemary

1 teaspoon dried oregano

Salt and black pepper to taste

Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

2.Prepare Vegetables:

In a large bowl, combine halved or quartered baby potatoes, baby carrots, and sliced zucchini.

3.Make Herb Mix:

In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.

4.Coat Vegetables:

Pour the herb mix over the vegetables and toss until the vegetables are evenly coated.

5.Roast in the Oven:

Spread the vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.

6.Roast:

Roast in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until the potatoes are golden brown and the vegetables are tender. Stir the vegetables halfway through the roasting time for even cooking.

7.Garnish:

Remove from the oven and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

8.Serve:

Serve the Garlic Herb Roasted Potatoes, Carrots, and Zucchini as a delicious side dish alongside your favorite main course.

Note:

These roasted vegetables are not only easy to make but also packed with flavor. Adjust the herbs and seasoning to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy your tasty and colorful side dish!

Broccoli Cashew Stir-Fry

This easy Broccoli Cashew Stir-Fry makes a satisfying 30-minute weeknight vegan meal! A healthy oil-free stir-fry with garlic & ginger.

Ingredients:

1 lb (about 450g) broccoli, cut into florets

1 cup unsalted cashews

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated

1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced

1 carrot, julienned

1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce

2 tablespoons oyster sauce

1 tablespoon hoisin sauce

1 tablespoon sesame oil

1 tablespoon rice vinegar

1 teaspoon sugar

2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)

Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

Cooked rice or noodles (for serving)

Instructions:

1.Blanch Broccoli:

Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the broccoli florets and cook for about 2 minutes, or until they are bright green and slightly tender. Drain and immediately transfer the broccoli to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain again and set aside.

2.Toast Cashews:

In a dry skillet over medium heat, toast the cashews until they are golden brown and fragrant. Stir frequently to prevent burning. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

3.Stir-Fry Vegetables:

Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, stir-frying for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

Add the sliced red and yellow bell peppers and julienned carrot. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.

4.Add Broccoli and Cashews:

Add the blanched broccoli and toasted cashews to the wok. Toss everything together.

5.Prepare Sauce:

In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and sugar.

6.Combine and Stir-Fry:

Pour the sauce over the vegetables and cashews. Toss everything together until well coated and heated through.

7.Garnish and Serve:

Garnish the stir-fry with sliced green onions and sesame seeds, if desired. Serve the broccoli cashew stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Note:

This Broccoli Cashew Stir-Fry is a flavorful and nutritious dish that comes together quickly. Adjust the sauce ingredients to your taste preference and enjoy this delicious and satisfying stir-fry!

Greek Salad

This easy Greek salad recipe is refreshing & delicious! Made with cucumber, tomatoes, olives, peppers, & feta, Greek salad is the best summer side dish.

Ingredients:

For the Salad:

4 cups cherry tomatoes, halved

1 cucumber, diced

1 red bell pepper, diced

1 yellow bell pepper, diced

1 red onion, thinly sliced

1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted

1 cup feta cheese, crumbled

1 cup cucumber, diced

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced

1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted

1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled

1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the Dressing:

1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil

2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

1 teaspoon dried oregano

Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

1.Prepare Vegetables:

In a large salad bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, diced red and yellow bell peppers, sliced red onion, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.

2.Make Dressing:

In a small bowl or jar, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.

3.Assemble Salad:

Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients.

4.Toss and Garnish:

Gently toss the salad until all ingredients are well coated with the dressing. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley on top.

5.Chill and Serve:

Chill the Greek salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Serve chilled.

Optional Additions:

Add slices of ripe cherry tomatoes.

Include a handful of crumbled feta cheese for extra creaminess.

Top with a sprinkle of dried oregano for added Greek flavor.

Note:

This Greek Salad is a delightful combination of fresh vegetables, briny olives, and creamy feta cheese, all tossed in a flavorful dressing. It's perfect as a side dish or a light and healthy main course. Enjoy!

Grilled Chicken Cobb Salad with Honey Dijon {Paleo}

This grilled chicken Cobb salad is packed with all the goodies! Perfectly seasoned grilled chicken, crispy bacon, sliced eggs and avocado plus an easy and delicious honey dijon dressing that's ready in under a minute!

Ingredients:

For the Salad:

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Salt and black pepper to taste

1 tablespoon olive oil

8 cups mixed salad greens (such as romaine lettuce and arugula)

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 cucumber, diced

1 avocado, sliced

4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced

6 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

1/2 cup crumbled paleo-friendly cheese (optional)

For the Honey Dijon Dressing:

1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

1 tablespoon honey (adjust to taste for sweetness)

1 clove garlic, minced

Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

1.Grill Chicken:

Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Season chicken breasts with salt and black pepper. Brush with olive oil. Grill for 6-8 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Allow the chicken to rest for a few minutes before slicing.

2.Prepare Salad Greens:

In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, avocado slices, hard-boiled eggs, crumbled bacon, and paleo-friendly cheese (if using).

3.Slice Chicken:

Slice the grilled chicken breasts into thin strips.

4.Assemble Cobb Salad:

Arrange the sliced chicken over the salad greens and vegetables.

5.Make Honey Dijon Dressing:

In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, honey, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper.

6.Dress Salad:

Drizzle the Honey Dijon dressing over the salad. Toss gently to coat all ingredients with the dressing.

7.Serve:

Serve the Grilled Chicken Cobb Salad immediately, garnished with extra bacon, avocado slices, and a sprinkle of black pepper if desired.

Note:

This Grilled Chicken Cobb Salad with Honey Dijon dressing is not only delicious but also fits into a Paleo diet. It's a satisfying and nutrient-rich meal perfect for lunch or dinner. Enjoy!
